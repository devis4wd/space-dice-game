Space Dice - Game Rules

  1. "Roll Dice" to roll the dice.
  2. If you roll a 1, you lose your current points.
  3. If you roll a 1, it's also the other Player's turn.
  4. If you roll above 1, your current score keeps increasing.
  5. "Hold Score" to save your current points to your total score.
  6. "Hold Score" also passes the turn to the other Player.
  7. The first Player to reach 100 points wins.
  8. "New Game" to start over.
